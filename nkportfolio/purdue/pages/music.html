Lullaby

This is a lullaby for solo piano that I composed in my free time. My goal was to maintain a soothing yet sophisticated melody (with occasional flurries of stylish dissonance) while sustaining a simple chord progression.

Plastic Bottle Percussion

This was one of my attempts at sampling. I was playing around with an empty 7-Up bottle after eating pizza one evening, when I was inspired by the sounds that it made. I felt that it had the potential to be used as a percussion instrument. I proceeded to record various different sounds from it, curate the samples, configure a multi-sampled (deep-sampled), velocity sensitive virtual instrument using the EXS24 sampler in Logic Pro X, and finally composed a song using the instrument to see what it was capable of (like a test drive, if you will). Here is the final result.
