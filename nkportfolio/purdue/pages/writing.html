Technical Comparison of Jazz and Carnatic

This essay, as the title clearly suggests, is a technical analysis and comparison of music, specifically jazz and carnatic music. Carnatic music is the name given to South Indian classical music. Having originated from completely different circumstances, these two genres of music seemed to be an intruiging pair to compare. That is what served as my inspiration to write this essay.
