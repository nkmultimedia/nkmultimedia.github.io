About Me

I love cartoons. Old cartoons. From the '30s through '60s. As much as I enjoy new content, I feel that there’s a certain spark in the classics. At the start of my career, I faced many situations that made me think, "Why do you need an artist to do that?". This question has guided me through many industries, from product design to software engineering. I'm currently pursuing an MS in Digital Production Arts at Clemson University, a program that balances art and technology. I hope to spend my career attempting to recapture that magic of old school animation, in whatever capacity possible. As an artist, as an engineer, whatever the role may be, I would like to contribute toward this journey. My craft is nowhere near perfect, but isn’t the quest for excellence a life long endeavor?
