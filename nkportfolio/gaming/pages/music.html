Music

Writing music has been another one of my hobbies for a long time, and I have recently begun self publishing as an independent artist. My debut single, Sublime, is a contemporary, semi-classical piano piece. Originally intended as a lullaby, this piece was initially written for solo piano. Now accompanied by an orchestral arrangement, I feel that this piece accurately reflects my artistic style. Sublime is now available for streaming and digital download on 35 major streaming platforms.

Film Scores

I have written films scores for over 10+ indie productions/short films and trailers encompassing 6 languages. One such example is JFC’s “Kantho”, a modern satirical take on the youth of this generation. Directed by Vishal Patnaik, this film is a coming of age comedy situated in Odisha, India, with a message and a relatable story as you can see these characters in your daily life.
After finishing the project, we decided to release aMashupcontaining extracts of the score to promote the film.Kantho Mashupis available for streaming on over700music platforms globally!

Tutorials

I love to share what I have learned, and I find making tutorials is a fun way to do this. For example, 32-bit audio is a topic that took me quite a while to understand, and I’ve met many musicians and engineers who have trouble with it. In this series of 2 tutorials, I breakdown 32-bit audio to cover concepts, application, and best practices. The first video covers the theory of how audio is digitized, and how this plays a role in the perceived quality of sound. The second video details real-world examples from my commissioned projects, when and when not to use 32 bits, along with best practices.
